100% Vegetarian Recipe Collection

Quick, nutrient-dense vegetarian meals you can actually repeat.

These egg-free, vegetarian options balance protein, fiber, and healthy fats while keeping prep manageable. All recipes include prep time, nutritional info, and simple instructions.

Breakfast Recipes

Power Breakfast Bowl

⏱️ 5 mins | 🔥 420 cal | 💪 18g protein

Ingredients: ½ cup oats, 1 cup Greek yogurt, 1 tbsp chia seeds, ½ cup mixed berries, 2 tbsp almonds, 1 tsp honey

Method: Mix oats with yogurt, top with chia seeds, berries, almonds, and drizzle honey. Serve cold or microwave for 1 minute.

Banana Oat Pancakes

⏱️ 12 mins | 🔥 320 cal | 💪 10g protein

Ingredients: 1 ripe banana, ½ cup oats, ¼ cup milk, ¼ tsp baking powder, cinnamon, cooking spray, maple syrup

Method: Blend banana, oats, milk, baking powder until smooth. Cook on medium heat for 2-3 mins per side. Serve with maple syrup and berries.

Avocado Toast Supreme

⏱️ 5 mins | 🔥 380 cal | 💪 16g protein

Ingredients: 2 slices whole grain bread, ½ avocado, 50g paneer slices, cherry tomatoes, pumpkin seeds, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes

Method: Toast bread, mash avocado on top, add grilled paneer slices and tomatoes. Sprinkle pumpkin seeds and season.

Lunch Recipes

Paneer Protein Wrap

⏱️ 10 mins | 🔥 420 cal | 💪 22g protein

Ingredients: 1 whole wheat wrap, 100g grilled paneer, 2 tbsp hummus, lettuce, cucumber, tomato, red onion, mint chutney

Method: Spread hummus on wrap, layer veggies and grilled paneer, drizzle mint chutney, season with salt and pepper, roll tightly and toast for 2 minutes.

Buddha Bowl

⏱️ 20 mins | 🔥 520 cal | 💪 22g protein

Ingredients: 1 cup quinoa, 150g chickpeas, 2 cups mixed greens, ½ avocado, shredded carrot, tahini dressing

Method: Cook quinoa, roast chickpeas at 400°F for 20 mins. Assemble bowl with greens, quinoa, chickpeas, avocado, carrots. Drizzle tahini.

Paneer Salad Power Bowl

⏱️ 10 mins | 🔥 360 cal | 💪 24g protein

Ingredients: 100g grilled paneer, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, chickpeas, olive oil, lemon juice, herbs

Method: Arrange greens in bowl, add grilled paneer cubes, chickpeas, and veggies. Dress with olive oil, lemon juice, and herbs.

Dinner Recipes

Tofu Veggie Stir-Fry

⏱️ 15 mins | 🔥 380 cal | 💪 18g protein

Ingredients: 150g firm tofu, broccoli, bell pepper, mushrooms, 2 cloves garlic, 1 tsp ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil

Method: Heat sesame oil, sauté garlic and ginger. Add cubed tofu, cook until golden. Add veggies, stir-fry 5 mins. Add soy sauce.

Baked Paneer & Veggies

⏱️ 25 mins | 🔥 420 cal | 💪 26g protein

Ingredients: 150g paneer cubes, bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, lemon, olive oil, garlic, Italian herbs, chili flakes

Method: Place paneer and veggies on baking sheet, drizzle olive oil, add minced garlic and herbs. Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes.

Lentil Soup

⏱️ 35 mins | 🔥 320 cal | 💪 18g protein

Ingredients: 1 cup red lentils, carrot, celery, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, cumin, turmeric, spinach

Method: Sauté onion, garlic, carrot, celery. Add lentils, broth, spices. Simmer 25 mins. Add spinach last 2 mins.

Healthy Snacks

Energy Balls

⏱️ 10 mins | 🔥 150 cal/ball | 💪 5g protein

Ingredients: 1 cup dates, ½ cup almonds, 2 tbsp cocoa powder, 1 tbsp chia seeds, pinch of salt

Method: Blend all ingredients, roll into 10-12 balls. Refrigerate for 30 mins. Store in fridge for up to 1 week.

Veggie Hummus Plate

⏱️ 5 mins | 🔥 220 cal | 💪 8g protein

Ingredients: ½ cup hummus, carrot sticks, cucumber slices, bell pepper strips, cherry tomatoes

Method: Arrange veggies on plate with hummus in center. Great for afternoon snacking or pre-workout fuel.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

⏱️ 3 mins | 🔥 280 cal | 💪 20g protein

Ingredients: 1 cup Greek yogurt, ¼ cup granola, ½ cup mixed berries, 1 tbsp honey, mint leaves

Method: Layer yogurt, granola, and berries in a glass. Drizzle honey on top and garnish with mint.

🎓 Hostel-Friendly Vegetarian Recipes

Quick, budget-friendly 100% vegetarian meals perfect for students with minimal equipment. No eggs, no fancy kitchen needed!

Spicy Peanut Noodles

⏱️ 7 mins | 🔥 360 cal | 💪 12g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹25

Equipment: Electric kettle only

Ingredients: 1 pack instant noodles, handful spinach, 1 tbsp peanut butter, 2 tbsp roasted peanuts, chili flakes, soy sauce

Method: Boil noodles in kettle. Drain most water, add peanut butter, soy sauce, spinach. Top with roasted peanuts and chili flakes.

Microwave Veg Upma

⏱️ 6 mins | 🔥 280 cal | 💪 8g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹20

Equipment: Microwave + bowl

Ingredients: ½ cup semolina (sooji), 1 cup water, chopped onion, tomato, green chili, 1 tsp oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, salt

Method: Mix all in bowl. Microwave 2 mins, stir. Microwave 2 more mins, stir. Add lemon juice. Done!

Peanut Butter Banana Wrap

⏱️ 3 mins | 🔥 420 cal | 💪 14g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹20

Equipment: None needed!

Ingredients: 1 chapati/tortilla, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 1 banana, 1 tsp honey, cinnamon powder

Method: Spread peanut butter on chapati, place sliced banana, drizzle honey, sprinkle cinnamon. Roll and eat!

Kettle Rice Bowl

⏱️ 12 mins | 🔥 420 cal | 💪 14g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹35

Equipment: Electric kettle

Ingredients: ½ cup instant rice, 1 packet soup mix, 50g paneer cubes, frozen mixed veggies, soy sauce, chili sauce

Method: Boil water in kettle. Pour over rice and soup mix in bowl. Add frozen veggies and paneer. Cover 5 mins. Top with sauces.

Microwave Veg Fried Rice

⏱️ 8 mins | 🔥 360 cal | 💪 10g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹35

Equipment: Microwave + bowl

Ingredients: 1 cup cooked rice, mixed veggies (carrot, peas, corn, beans), soy sauce, garlic powder, oil, green onions, sesame seeds

Method: Mix rice, veggies, oil in bowl. Microwave 2 mins, stir. Microwave 2 more mins. Add soy sauce, garlic, sesame seeds. Mix well.

Bread Pizza

⏱️ 6 mins | 🔥 320 cal | 💪 16g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹30

Equipment: Microwave or sandwich maker

Ingredients: 2 bread slices, 2 tbsp ketchup, cheese slice, corn, capsicum, oregano, chili flakes

Method: Spread ketchup on bread, add toppings and cheese. Microwave 1 min or toast in sandwich maker until cheese melts.

Overnight Oats (No Cook)

⏱️ 2 mins + soak | 🔥 350 cal | 💪 15g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹25

Equipment: Just a container

Ingredients: ½ cup oats, ½ cup curd/yogurt, ½ cup milk, 1 tbsp chia seeds, banana, honey, nuts

Method: Mix oats, curd, milk, chia seeds in container. Refrigerate overnight. Add banana, honey, nuts in morning. Ready!

Paneer Bhurji (Microwave)

⏱️ 6 mins | 🔥 290 cal | 💪 18g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹40

Equipment: Microwave + bowl

Ingredients: 100g crumbled paneer, onion, tomato, green chili, 1 tsp oil, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, salt

Method: Mix all ingredients in bowl. Microwave 1 min, stir. Microwave another 1.5 mins, stir. Serve with bread.

Chickpea Salad Bowl

⏱️ 5 mins | 🔥 340 cal | 💪 14g protein | 💰 Budget: ₹35

Equipment: None needed!

Ingredients: 1 cup boiled chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, onion, lemon juice, chaat masala, coriander, salt

Method: Chop all veggies, mix with chickpeas. Add lemon juice, chaat masala, salt. Toss well. Instant healthy meal!

🎒 Hostel Kitchen Essentials

🔌 Must-Have: Electric kettle (most versatile), microwave-safe containers, small knife, spoon/fork set

🛒 Smart Shopping: Buy in bulk with roommates. Store dry goods in airtight containers. Keep paneer and curd stocked.

💡 Pro Tips: Befriend the mess cook for hot water access. Share appliances. Keep instant meals for busy days.

🧊 Storage Hacks: Use curd cups for storage. Zip-lock bags save space. Label everything with date.

Meal Prep Tips

🥗 Batch Cook

Prepare grains (rice, quinoa) and proteins ahead on Sunday. Store in portions for easy assembly throughout the week.

🔪 Pre-Chop Veggies

Wash and chop vegetables for the week. Store in airtight containers to save 10+ minutes per meal.

📦 Smart Storage

Use glass containers with compartments. Label with dates. Most prepped meals last 3-4 days in the fridge.

🔄 Rotate Flavors

Keep base ingredients simple but vary spices and sauces. Same protein + different seasoning = new meal.
