💡 How to Use This Chart

📊 Track Your Macros

Use this chart to log your meals in our Nutrition Tracker. Aim for balanced macros based on your goals.

🎯 Weight Loss

Focus on high-protein, high-fiber foods. Target 400-500 cal per meal with 20-25g protein.

💪 Muscle Gain

Prioritize protein (30-40g per meal) and carbs. Aim for 600-700 cal per meal with weight training.

⚖️ Maintenance

Balance your intake with moderate portions. Target 500-600 cal per meal with 20-30g protein.
