Meal Strategy

Diet guide library for realistic daily eating.

Use these starter frameworks and adapt portions to your goals. Consistency beats intensity.

Balanced Plate

Half vegetables, quarter protein, quarter complex carbs. Good for general health and sustainable weight control.

High Protein Day

Prioritize lean proteins in each meal for satiety, muscle support, and appetite control.

Light Evening Plan

Front-load calories earlier in the day and keep dinner fiber-rich, protein-focused, and lower in fat.
